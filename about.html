About Me

This about "us" is really about "me", my name is Saba Zelalem and I am the sole writer for Abstract Economics. I am currently a senior in highschool, whos passionate about going into finance and running this blog to entertain others on the finance world.

Purpose

I started Abstract Economics because I saw a problem during my sophomore year of highschool. I wanted to see what the "finance world" was like but a lot of the articles I was trying to read were outdated or hard to read, they used words that were meant for people who were already heavily invested in finance. So that's where Abstract Economics comes in. It breaks down recent topics in finance and tries to explain them in a concise beginner friendly way so that anyone who's interested in finance can explore.
